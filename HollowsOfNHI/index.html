"A Disclosure Rogue"

Traditional roguelike where you enter the secret coverup complex in hopes of exposing the UFO conspiracy. Descend into a dark procedural complex teeming with secrets, symbols, and non-human intelligences. Find The Archives of Disclosure and use them to escape and expose the truth for the whole world - or die trying. An adaptation of timeless classic set in modern technology era. Now also with new graphics, sounds, ambience and music.

Touch screen friendly

Swipe to move around, pinch to zoom, drag to pan around the dungeon. There's also virtual d-pad, and zoom and pan can be used with single finger only. Supports both portrait and landscape. Use all hero commands via modal menus interactions. Has several 'quick' buttons for common actions and new command to navigate automatically to the exit.

Tiles by Oryx
